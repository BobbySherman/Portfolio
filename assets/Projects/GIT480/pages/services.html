Services

Delivery

Sheridan Lumber delivers to all of Peoria, IL and the surrounding area. We'll deliver materials for your new Home, Deck, Addition, Windows, or Doors. Our drivers love to help and will carry or drop off the materials next to the work area. Sheridan Lumber believes in fair delivery prices. Call us for an estimate.

Custom Screen Rooms

Sheridan Lumber offers custom screen rooms. We will measure, design, and install a screen room prefect for your deck, porch, or patio. Call us to discuss your new screen room and for a free estimate.

Wood Shop

Sheridan Lumber's wood shop can with you next project. We provide the following services:

  • custom wood work
  • sheeting ripping
  • pre cutting
  • routing
  • custom crates

Take Off's

Sheridan Lumber knows that contractors are juggling multiple jobs. We want to help you by taking a task off your todo list. We will generate material list and coordinate deliveries for the different stages of your projects. Just drop off the blueprints and tell us the start date. Stop by to speak with our sales staff.

Door Shop

Sheridan Lumber can modify, customize, and build doors for your next project. As a house ages door ways tend to shift. This can cause a door to bind on the jams or drag on the floor. We can take a look at issues like this and make professional adjustments.

Senco Gun Repair

Sheridan Lumber knows that nail guns take a beating on the job site. If you're experiencing any difficulties with your nail gun drop it off at the store and our Senco specialist with either fix the issue in house or send it off to the supplier for more serious issues.

Screen Repair

Sheridan Lumber provides on site and in store screen repairs. Just drop off small window screens and sliding door screens in store. For those large or hard to reach screens call us and we will send a knowledgeable repair man to fix you screen.
