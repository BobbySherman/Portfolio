Security Tips

Diversify and secure your passwords
pcmag.com
Use a dedicated service with an encrypted password vault.
Keep Track of who's tracking you
digitaltrends.com
Use an extension like Privacy Badger (eff.org) to see where your information is going
Think about your actions
norton.com
Think before you click on something and don't sign into sensitive websites on public networks

Your privacy is your responsibility. Anonymous internet activity is very difficult to achieve. So watch your accounts, report suspicious activity, and keep your products updated.
